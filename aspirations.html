Personal Aspirations

Progress over perfection • One step at a time

🌱 My Zero-Waste Goals

♻️

Reusable Containers

I carry reusable containers daily – from food to shopping – to avoid single-use plastics. Even takeaways go in my own containers!

🌿

Composting Food Waste

I started composting fruit and vegetable scraps at home using a DIY compost bin. It's small-scale, but it reduces a lot of my daily trash!

🧼

Natural, Refillable Products

I'm transitioning to solid soaps, refillable shampoos, and toothpaste tablets to cut down on plastic bottles and tubes.

🚧 The Struggles Are Real

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Challenge: Family Habits
My family still prefers plastic bags and disposables. It's tough to change routines.
✅ Solution:
I asked them to try reusable alternatives for just 30 days. Small wins like using reusable bottles or cloth bags make a difference!
🏪 Challenge: Convenience
Sometimes I forget my containers or it's just quicker to grab packaged snacks.
✅ Solution:
I now keep a 'zero-waste kit' (bag, box, water bottle, etc.) in my backpack and my bike!
💰 Challenge: Cost
Eco-products can feel expensive upfront.
✅ Solution:
I started with second-hand jars and containers. I reuse milk cartons too!

🌟 My Mission

My goal isn't perfection ‐ it's progress. By rethinking waste and choosing better habits, I believe I can reduce my yearly waste to under 100kg and inspire others along the way. Every small action creates ripples of change.
